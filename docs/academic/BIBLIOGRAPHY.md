## Citations in Academic Format

Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A. N., Kaiser, Ł., & Polosukhin, I. (2017). Attention is all you need. In *Advances in neural information processing systems* (Vol. 30). Curran Associates, Inc.

## Note on Sources

This bibliography includes works that directly influenced the development of RafaelIA, as well as foundational texts in relevant fields. Not all works are explicitly cited in the main documentation, but all contributed to the theoretical framework and implementation decisions.

The interdisciplinary nature of RafaelIA requires synthesis across multiple domains. Readers are encouraged to explore the intersections between these fields rather than treating them in isolation.
